Mission Statement

Our mission statement is to educate kids about electronics at an early age, and reduce electric waste by accepting donations.

We are a non-profit where parents can bring kids into our facilities to get lessons on how to fix their electronic goods and get their hands on repairing electronic and hardware.

Where We're From

We are based out of the greater area of Charlotte, North Carolina.
